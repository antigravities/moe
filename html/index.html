Abuse policy (because people can't just be normal and rule-abiding)

To prevent the classic "you didn't warn me" meme, here is the policy regarding abuse: don't

This includes, but is in no way limited to:
  1. Excessive or unwanted spam
  2. Attempting to evade bans or blocks towards a certain user
  3. Violating any written and/or confirmed rule(s) set by Groupees staff or myself
  4. Violating the law.
If you think what you're doing could possibly get you banned, don't.

Depending on the severity, actions deemed abusive may result in a permanent or temporary ban from moe or Groupees. We reserve the right to amend these rules and/or ban any user, at any time, for any reason.
